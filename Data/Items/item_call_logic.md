For each item the "Category" has these possible values: 
1. "weapon"
2. "consumable"
3. "key_item"
4. "stun_item"
5. "environment"
6. "upgrade_item" (usable for health upgrade or weapon damage upgrade)


Along with that they have different effects to suit the item
1. "weapon_stats" → weapons only
2. "consumable_effects" → consumables only
3. "interaction" → key items, stun items, environmental
